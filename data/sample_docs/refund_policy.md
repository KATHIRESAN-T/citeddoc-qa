# Refund Policy

Customers may request a refund within 14 calendar days of purchase when onboarding has
not started and less than 20 percent of the included usage credits have been consumed.

Refunds are not available for custom integration work, training workshops, or prepaid
enterprise support retainers. Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment
method within seven business days.

If a customer has a billing dispute, the support team must create a billing-review ticket
and attach the invoice, payment reference, and customer communication history.
