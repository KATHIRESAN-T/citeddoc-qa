# Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval. Fully remote
work requires approval from the department head and People Operations.

Employees working remotely must be reachable during core collaboration hours from 10:00 AM
to 4:00 PM local time. Sensitive customer data may only be accessed through the company VPN
and approved devices.

Exceptions for medical, family, or relocation reasons are reviewed by People Operations.
